Hesperia Haunt: Halloween Decorating Contest 2024

Welcome to the spookiest contest in Hesperia! Join if you dare and make your home the creepiest on the block! This is the first annual Home decorating contest. Please submit your request to join by 10/19/2024. Our contest will be judged and winner will be announced on 10/23/2024. After submitting your request, your home will be added to a list of homes in the area so that the community can go out and enjoy a spoooky tour. Our winning home will be featured on our welcome page for the remaining of the year and will have braging rights.

Contest Details

To register your home for the competition, simply click the link below and send us an email with your address and any eerie decoration details. After receving your request, we will ask for pictures to enter into our website. A map with all the homes in the contest will be made available for the public to go out and take a spooooky tour.
